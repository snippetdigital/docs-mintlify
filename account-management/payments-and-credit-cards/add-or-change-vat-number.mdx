---
title: "Add or change VAT number"
---

If you want to add your VAT number to your invoices; 

Click the user icon in your top right-hand corner, and select -> Billing settings -> Manage billing settings.

<img src="/images/Image 2022-09-28 at 1.19.59 PM.jpg" alt="" />

Click "Update information."

<img src="/images/Image 2022-09-28 at 1.18.10 PM.jpg" alt="" />

Under TAX ID. Select your country. 

Add the VAT number. 

Click "Save".

<img src="/images/Image 2022-09-28 at 1.18.57 PM.jpg" alt="" />
