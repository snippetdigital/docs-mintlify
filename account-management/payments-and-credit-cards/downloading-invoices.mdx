### How to download an invoice?

There are multiple ways to download an invoice. 

Click the user icon in your top right-hand corner, and select -> Invoices. 

<img src="/images/Keyword Insights 2023-07-30 at 8.45.19 PM.jpg" alt="" />

Click to download invoices.

<img src="/images/Keyword Insights 2023-07-30 at 8.46.51 PM.jpg" alt="" />

Click the user icon on your top right-hand corner, select -> Billing settings -> Manage billing settings -> Download under "Invoice history."

<img src="/images/Keyword Insights 2023-07-30 at 8.47.52 PM.jpg" alt="" />

<img src="/images/Keyword Insights 2023-07-30 at 8.48.48 PM.jpg" alt="" />

<img src="/images/Image 2022-09-28 at 1.17.07 PM.jpg" alt="" />

Please note that using the second method is not recommended, as Stripe will expire invoices after a certain period. Always use the first method, as we will locally host your invoices, and they will never expire.
