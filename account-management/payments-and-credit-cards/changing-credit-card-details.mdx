---
title: "Changing credit card details"
---

If you want to change your credit card details; 

Click the user icon in your top right-hand corner, and select -> Billing settings -> Manage billing settings.

Click the edit icon next to your default payment method. 

<img src="/images/Image 2022-09-28 at 1.27.06 PM.jpg" alt="" />

Follow the instructions to add a new card.
