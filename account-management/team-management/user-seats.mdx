With our user seats feature you can invite you entire team and collaborate on your projects. 

### How do i buy seats?

Go to Settings -> Click the + icon next to seats, select the number of seats and click buy.

<img src="/images/CleanShot 2025-02-12 at 17.11.09@2x.png" alt="" />

### What is the cost for user seats?

Each seat costs $10 per month. 

So if you buy 2 seats, it will be an additional $20 a month. 



<Info>

Tip: Upgrading to a higher plan with more seats can often be more cost-effective than purchasing additional seats individually. Visit our pricing comparison [page](https://www.keywordinsights.ai/pricing/) for full details.

</Info>
